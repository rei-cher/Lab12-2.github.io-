Using Rocks and Stones in Landscape Design

Building a Rock Garden

Rock gardens are very popular for today's landscaping projects. Rock gardens provide a natural look and feel to any landscape, and are low maintainance compared to plants. Rocks and stones that are native to your area are a best choice. Mix and match the rocks with different sizes and features. Large irregular rocks can be very interesting. Using soil, dirt, and adding flowers can give the rock garden a natural look and feel.
